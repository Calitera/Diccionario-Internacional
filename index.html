Our Mission / Nostra Mission

Our mission is to systemize, modernize and promote the Internacional language. We base our linguistic work on the norms dictated by João Evangelista Campos Lima, the language's creator.
La nostra mission è sistematizar, modernizar e promover la língua Internacional. Basamos la nostra labor linguistica súper las normas dictatas per João Evangelista Campos Lima.

Mission

Systematization / Sistematizacion

We use Campos Lima's system as our basis and expand the vocabulary upon it, loyal to his original criteria.

Nos usamos lo sistema formato per Campos Lima como nostra base e expandimos lo vocabulário súper ela, leales a lo suo critério original.

Modernization / Modernizacion

We adapt the language to the requirements of contemporary science and technology, strictly maintaining its etymological roots and Latin character.

Nos adaptamos la língua a las exigéncias de la sciéncia e tecnologia contemporáneas, mantenendo strictamente las suas radices etimológicas e lo carácter latino.

Promotion / Promocion

We propagate the language through education and literary production to demonstrate its fluency and absolute viability as a national and global medium.

Nos propagamos la língua através de la educacion e produccion literária pro demonstrar sua fluéncia e absoluta viabilitate como médio nacional e global.
